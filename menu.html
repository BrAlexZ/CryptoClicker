Crypto Clicker

GOAL:

The goal of the game is to get as many TOKENS as you can.
You can get TOKENS by clicking on the Coin and purchasing assets like miners, earners etc. to produce them.
The game has AutoSave. You can also save by clicking and reset your game anytime.

THE GAME:

During the game you will get new upgrades like Double Click which you can buy. They will help you achieve your goal quicker.
You will also recieve achievements which are only aesthetic badges which tells you your progress.
